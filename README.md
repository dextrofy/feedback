# Dextrofy Feedback

Welcome to Dextrofy's community feedback repository.

This is the central place to share ideas, report issues, and 
track what we are building next — for farmers, FPO managers, 
buyers, and developers.

---

## Our Products

| Product | What it does |
|---|---|
| 👁️ **Watch & Ward** | Drone-based crop intelligence — know what's happening in your field and how to fix it |
| 🛒 **Dextrofy Market** | Verified produce marketplace — sell with a crop passport, get premium prices |
